Pilau recipe

Pilau is a cuisine from East Africa. It is mostly enjoyed when one as guests or just on a casual afternoon or evening. Pilau comes in different f;lavours. There is chicken pilau or beef pilau we shall focus on chicken pilau in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 cups of Rice
  • 1kg chicken
  • pilau masala
  • salt to taste
  • one red onion
  • 8 cloves
  • tomato paste(however not a must)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic and tangawizi

Instructions

  1. Start by adding vegetable oil to the cooking pot. Add onion,garlic and ginger and cook until the onions caramelized
  2. Add the rest of the spices such as the pilau masala
  3. Add the chicken, ensure that the chicken is slighly boiled first before adding it this helps in tenderising the chicken. cook the chicken until golden brown.
  4. Add water , salt and the rice
  5. Once boiled , serve it with kachumbari and a drink of your choice
